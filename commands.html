Commands

Command Function Usage
choice Get random answer from selected choices ty!choice [option 1] [option 2] ...
or
/choice [option 1] [option 2] ...
dice Get random result from throwing a dice ty!dice [amount (optional)] or /dice [amount (optional)]
coin Get random result from flipping a coin ty!coin [amount (optional)] or /coin [amount (optional)]
answer Get random answer of a question from selected choices ty!answer [question] | [choice 1] [choice 2] ...
or
/answer [question] | [choice] [choice 2] ...
8ball Ask 8ball about your condition ty!8ball [question] or /8ball [question]
say Make Tycha say something ty!say [sentences] or /say [sentences]

avatar Get discord member avatar ty!avatar [@member] or /avatar [@member]
